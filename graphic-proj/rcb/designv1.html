Riverside Concert Band Commission

The Riverside Concert Band commissioned me to create a redesign for their business card to promote themselves for bookings and theirperformances.

Working together with their board members, the first concept aimed to create welcoming tone, encouraging people to join the band or hire them for their events. The musical staff in the background illiciting movement, excitement, and dynamicism. However, we came to the conclusion that it made the design too busy, simplifiying it to create more focus on the band's logo and their ideneity as a band.
