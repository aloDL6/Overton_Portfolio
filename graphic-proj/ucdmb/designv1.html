UC Davis Marching Band Shirt Design

The UC Davis Marching Band always designs a Picnic Day shirt to providing funding and to celebrate the Battle of the Bands event that we host. For 2026, I designed this years' design encompassing the years' theme, "The Journey Home: Destination Davis".

Working with leadership and the university marketing team to abide by university brand guidelines, I created this design to capture a sense of community and nostalgia. The design features subtle recognizable imagery of the university such as the water tower. Additionally, the main imagery uses a stamp and postcard to demonstrate the town as a travel desitination, just like the many schools who travel to the Picnic Day event. Moreover, the texturing of the text furthers this notion of vintage, authenticity, and sincerity
