Disney Infographic Project

In high school, we were tasked to create an informational infographic poster of anything were were passionate and interested in. For me, it was Disneyland trips. I always loved showing people around the park, pointing out hidden mickeys, and generally helping people I knew plan out their trips. So, for my project, I made a poster regarding some basic tips for planning a trip to the park.

My imagery includes references to Disney properties such as Toy Story with the cloud background and the Alice in Wonderland pocketwatch. However, the overall styling is flat, but colorful to provide simplicity and approachability. Additionally, I wanted the poster to feel like it could fit on the Disneyland app, as much of the styling takes inspiration from the app itself, as well as the general UI of the app's capabilities.
